About me

I am a seasoned coder looking to begin a career in software engineering. When I was a senior at Boston University, I took two introductory-level computer science courses, a course on Java and a course on data structures and algorithms. After graduating, I lived in Taiwan for a year to study Mandarin Chinese, which I speak fluently. More recently, I have done a number of open source coding projects. These include a machine learning project in Python and a backend refactoring of a bibliography management tool. The tool, JabRef, is maintained my many contributors around the world. In addition, from April to July 2020, I attended General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive, a coding bootcamp teaching in-demand web development skills.
